Who Are We?

Deadecor is a modern Algerian platform that brings together everything related to the world of construction, decoration, renovation and home equipment under one roof. We believe that beauty begins at home, and that every house deserves an elegant, smart and accessible touch.

Our Vision:
Connect suppliers, workshops, stores and brands from different regions (Algeria, Libya, Tunisia, Turkey...) directly to consumers - through a modern, simple and effective digital platform.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is DeaDecor Hub? ▾
DeaDecor Hub is an innovative platform and shopping center that combines digital and physical marketing for decoration products, furniture, home equipment and construction materials. We aim to connect suppliers and consumers in one place combining quality, diversity and simplicity.
Can I buy products directly from the platform? ▾
Yes, you can browse the digital catalog and order products directly from our approved suppliers through our platform. We provide detailed information, photos and prices for each product.
Does the platform display only Algerian products? ▾
We prioritize high-quality Algerian products, but the platform is also open to international products that meet our quality and design criteria.
Can I as an artisan or supplier display my products on DeaDecor Hub? ▾
Absolutely! We welcome supplier, artisan and manufacturer partners. You can register as an exhibitor and easily upload your products through your personal account.
Is there a physical exhibition space in addition to the digital platform? ▾
Yes, DeaDecor Hub offers a dual experience combining digital navigation and physical viewing through a complete shopping center allowing visitors to see products directly and interact with exhibitors.
How can I contact the support team? ▾
You can contact us via the contact form on the website, or by email and phone available on the "Contact Us" page. Our team is always ready to answer your questions.
