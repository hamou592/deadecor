Touch of Goodness – A Decor Project with Impact

"Touch of Goodness" is a humanitarian initiative by Deadecor, delivering a simple product (chair, table, door, curtains...) to a family in need, while documenting the story in a touching, respectful, and elegant way.

We select real cases (widows, orphans, humble families), and turn their challenging moments into moments of beauty that inspire dignity and hope.

Every week, we feature a new story, a new product, and a provider contributing to goodness in their own way.

Deadecor doesn’t just sell… it leaves an impact.

Nominate a Case - "A Touch of Good" Campaign

Do you know a family truly in need of a table, door, or essential equipment? Nominate them and we’ll contact you if they are selected.

Important Note Before Submitting a Nomination:

The "Lamsat Khair" initiative aims to provide a simple intervention with a meaningful impact. It is not intended for home renovations or financial assistance.

We focus on delivering tangible items – for example: refrigerator, air conditioner, wardrobe, stove, curtains, decorative touch... – as long as the request is realistic, feasible, and makes a real difference in the family's comfort.

Each intervention is carefully selected based on the case and the available budget. Through this initiative, we aim to offer a moment of dignity and a modest yet meaningful improvement.

If the case is truly in need and the request is reasonable, we would be happy to receive your nomination through the form.

At Deadecor, we believe that beauty begins with a simple touch.
